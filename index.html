A funny story

Once upon a time when I was such a young girl attending the university. An embarrassing event happened which I still feel embarrassed every time I remember it. One day I went to the campus and from far away I saw two of my friends (they were very close and always went everywhere together). I ran to them and hugged one of them. But when I looked at the face, I realized I made a mistake and to hide my embarrassment, I jumped to the one next to her and hugged her instead, thinking she must have been the right one. It turned out she was also not my friend. I was so embarrassed that I just wanted to disappear then and there, especially when my real friends were just a few meters away seeing everything.
